## What MUST Happen

### THE 4 COMPONENTS

1. **Return and Report:**
   - What was completed this week?
   - Fire Map Hits status

2. **Lessons Learned:**
   - What worked?
   - What didn't work?

3. **Course Correction:**
   - What needs to change?
   - Adjustments to Focus/Fire Maps

4. **New Targets:**
   - What's next week's Fire Map?

**Outcome:** Updated Focus Map, Updated Fire Map, Weekly Review Log
